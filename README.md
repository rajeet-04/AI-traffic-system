# AI-traffic-system

**Traffic signal and light detection and decision making**

To introduce students to ADAS perception and decision-making pipelines
To design and implement agentic AI systems following the sense–decide–act paradigm
To develop multi-agent collaborative perception systems
To integrate Explainable AI (XAI) for safety, transparency, and trust
To produce a research paper and/or deployable prototype within a short duration

Task	Baseline Models	Datasets
Lane Detection	U-Net, SCNN	TuSim le CULane
Object Detection	YOLOv8 / YOLOv12	BDDIOOK, COCO
Pothole Detection	YOLOv8-Seg	Custom Indian road data, IDD
Traffic Sign Recognition	ResNet18, CNN	GTSRB
Pedestrian Detection	YOLOv12	Cityscapes

## Decision and Coordination Agents
#### Time-To-CoIlision (TTC) estimation
#### Risk scoring using confidence-weighted fusion
#### Rule-based or lightweight ML-based arbitration

## Explainability Modules
#### Grad-CAM for CNN-based decisions
#### Attention heatmaps for region importance
#### Confidence and uncertainty visualization
#### Agent disagreement explanation


## Evaluation Metrics
####  Computer Vision Metrics
####  Mean Average Precision (mAP)
####  Intersection over Union (IOU)
####  Precision and Recall
####  Frames Per Second (FPS)
####  Inference Latency

## Multi-Agent System Metrics
#### Coordination gain (MAS vs single-agent)
#### Agent agreement rate
#### Fault tolerance under agent failure
#### Communication overhead

## Explainable AI Metrics
#### Faithfulness of explanations
#### Localization accuracy of heatmaps
#### Explanation stability
#### Human interpretability (qualitative)
#### Confidence calibration
